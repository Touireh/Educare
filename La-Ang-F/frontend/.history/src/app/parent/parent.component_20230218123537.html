EduCare

Managing children with special needs can be a challenge for parents and educators. Parents may have difficulty keeping track of their child's daily activities..

About us

The "EduCare" app aims to address these challenges by providing a platform for parents to track their child's daily activities at the daycare.

view the comments and observations of the educators, view the meal menus and daycare schedules.

and communicate in real-time with the educators.

Physical Health Support Services

Physical therapy, rehabilitation, nursing and support services for specific medical needs are used to help students with specific physical needs maintain good physical health.

Learning Support Services

Reading Assistance, Tutoring, Remediation, Basic Skills Development and Math Support services are used to help students understand the subjects being taught.
